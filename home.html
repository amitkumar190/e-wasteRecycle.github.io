Introduction about the company

At Remine India, we are passionate about creating a cleaner and greener environment by recycling e-Waste and Li Batteries. As a recognized startup in the field of sustainable waste management, we are committed to providing reliable and cost-effective recycling solutions to businesses and households across India. Our mission is to help our clients minimize their carbon footprint and contribute to a more sustainable future. We believe that recycling e-Waste and Li Batteries is not just a responsibility but an opportunity to create value by recovering precious metals and other materials that can be reused.

What We Do

We specialize in the collection, transportation, and recycling of e-Waste and Li Batteries. Our state-of-the-art recycling facility is equipped with the latest technology to safely and efficiently process various types of electronic waste and Li Batteries.

Our services include:
    E-waste Recycling: We recycle all types of electronic waste, including computers, laptops, mobile phones, printers, and more.
    Li Battery Recycling: We recycle all types of Li batteries, including laptop batteries, phone batteries, power bank batteries, and more.
    Data Destruction: We ensure secure data destruction to protect your confidential information.
    Corporate Solutions: We offer customized solutions for businesses looking to recycle their electronic waste in a sustainable and responsible manner.
Our Commitment to Sustainability

At Remine India, we are committed to creating a cleaner and greener environment for present and future generations. We understand the impact that electronic waste and Li batteries can have on our environment, and we are determined to reduce this impact by providing sustainable recycling solutions. Our aim is to recover valuable materials from electronic waste and Li batteries, and turn them into new products, thus reducing the need for mining and manufacturing of new resources. By working with us, you can be assured that your electronic waste and Li batteries will be handled responsibly, and recycled in an environmentally friendly manner. Join us in our mission to make India a cleaner and more sustainable place.
